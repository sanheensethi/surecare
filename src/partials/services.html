Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about appointments, lab reports, specialist hours and payments.

Walk-ins are welcome for general consultations during our opening hours. However, for specialist consultations — especially Urology with Dr. Ashwin Giridhar, and visiting super-specialist consultants — we strongly recommend booking ahead. You can book via our appointment page, call +91 99889 96188, or message us on WhatsApp.

Yes. Our in-house pharmacy is located right at the clinic, so you can collect all prescribed medicines immediately after your consultation — no second trip needed. You can also enquire about medicines on WhatsApp.

Most routine tests — such as CBC, Lipid Profile, LFT, KFT, Thyroid and HbA1c — are typically ready the same day or by the next day. Cultures and specialised tests take a little longer. We'll always give you an expected report time when you book.

Dr. Ashwin Giridhar, our Consultant Urologist, Transplant Surgeon & Uro-Oncologist, is available at Surecare Clinic from 6 PM–8 PM, Monday to Friday. Super-specialist consultations are by appointment — please contact us and we'll arrange a suitable slot for you.

We accept cash, credit and debit cards, and UPI / online payments — for consultations, pharmacy purchases and lab tests alike.
