Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about appointments, delivery, lab reports and payments.

Walk-ins are welcome for general consultations during our opening hours. However, for our specialist consultations — especially Urology with Dr. Ashwin Giridhar, and visiting Neurology & Nephrology consultants — we strongly recommend booking ahead so we can reserve your slot. You can book online via our appointment page, call +91 99889 96188, or message us on WhatsApp.

We offer home delivery of medicines across Yelahanka. Charges, if any, depend on your location and order size — for most nearby addresses, delivery is free. The simplest way is to send your prescription on WhatsApp and our pharmacy team will confirm availability and delivery details right away.

Most routine tests — such as CBC, Lipid Profile, LFT, KFT, Thyroid and HbA1c — are typically ready the same day or by the next day. Cultures and certain specialised tests take a little longer as they require incubation. We'll always give you an expected report time when you book or drop off your sample.

Dr. Ashwin Giridhar, our Consultant Urologist, Transplant Surgeon & Uro-Oncologist, is available at Surecare Clinic from 6 PM–8 PM, Monday to Friday. Our Neurology and Nephrology consultations are provided by visiting specialists on an appointment-on-request basis — please contact us and we'll arrange a suitable date and time for you.

We accept cash, credit and debit cards, and UPI / online payments — for consultations, pharmacy purchases and lab tests alike. Choose whatever is most convenient for you.
