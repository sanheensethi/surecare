Who We Are

Complete Care Under One Roof, in the Heart of Yelahanka

Surecare Clinic is a multispeciality clinic built around one simple idea: your consultation, your medicines and your lab tests should all happen in one trusted place.

Located on 8th B Cross Road, opposite Indira Canteen in Yelahanka New Town, Surecare Clinic brings together experienced specialists, a full-service in-house pharmacy and an on-site diagnostics lab. Patients no longer need to travel from clinic to chemist to lab — everything is handled under one roof, by one caring team.

Our flagship strengths are Urology, Neurology and Nephrology, supported by a broad range of departments including General Medicine, Pediatrics, Gynecology, Dermatology, Orthopedics, Cardiology, Diabetology, Pulmonology and ENT. From a routine check-up to advanced surgical care, we put patients first at every step.

  • Consultations, pharmacy & diagnostics in a single visit
  • Home sample collection for lab tests
  • Open 7 days a week with transparent, affordable pricing

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, high-quality and compassionate healthcare to every family in Yelahanka by uniting expert consultations, dependable medicines and accurate diagnostics in one convenient location — with honesty, transparency and the patient always at the centre.

Our Vision

To be Yelahanka’s most trusted neighbourhood multispeciality clinic — recognised for clinical excellence in Urology, Neurology and Nephrology, and for a seamless, one-roof care experience that saves patients time, worry and travel.
